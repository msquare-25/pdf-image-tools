How to Convert JPG to PNG (Online)

Turn your JPG images into PNGs in a few taps — no app install, no signup.

When should you use PNG instead of JPG?

PNG is better when you need transparency (no background), sharper edges for text and logos, or want to avoid repeated compression on an image.

Step-by-step: Convert JPG to PNG

  1. Open the JPG → PNG tool.
  2. Upload one or more JPG images.
  3. Wait a moment while the tool converts them to PNG.
  4. Download the converted PNG files (or ZIP if there are many).

Tips for better results

  • Start with the highest-quality JPG you have for cleaner PNG output.
  • Use Crop PDF or image cropping tools before conversion if you need to trim.
  • Remember that PNG files are usually larger than JPG, because they store more detail.

All conversions run inside your browser — your images stay on your device.
