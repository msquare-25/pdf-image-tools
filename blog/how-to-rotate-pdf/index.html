How to Rotate a PDF (Any Device)

Fix upside-down pages or rotate the entire document using a fast, free browser tool.

Why Rotate a PDF?

Scanned documents or exported files often come out sideways or completely upside down. Instead of re-scanning, you can rotate pages instantly in your browser.

Step-by-step: Rotate PDF pages

  1. Open the Rotate PDF tool.
  2. Upload your PDF.
  3. Select any page and choose Rotate Left or Rotate Right.
  4. Apply the same rotation to all pages if needed.
  5. Download the rotated PDF.

Common Use Cases

  • Scanned pages appear sideways
  • Camera-captured documents rotate incorrectly
  • Exported PDFs from apps have mixed orientations
  • Forms scanned upside-down

Tips for a cleaner PDF

  • Use Crop PDF to fix extra margins
  • Use Compress PDF to reduce file size
  • Use Delete pages to remove unwanted pages

Everything runs in your browser — your PDF never leaves your device.
