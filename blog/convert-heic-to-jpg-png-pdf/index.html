How to Convert HEIC to JPG, PNG or PDF (iPhone Photos)

Quickly convert your HEIC photos to common formats using a fast and free browser tool — no app required.

Why are iPhone photos in HEIC?

Apple uses HEIC because it saves space while keeping high quality. But many websites, apps and desktops don’t support HEIC properly, so converting them to JPG, PNG or PDF becomes necessary.

Convert HEIC to JPG (Recommended)

  1. Open the JPG → PNG / JPG Converter.
  2. Upload your HEIC photo.
  3. The tool automatically converts it to JPG.
  4. Download the new JPG file.

Convert HEIC to PNG (Better for quality)

  1. Open the PNG Converter.
  2. Upload your HEIC photo.
  3. Download the converted PNG file.

Convert HEIC to PDF (Documents & Uploads)

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool.
  2. Upload your HEIC image.
  3. Choose page size (A4 works best).
  4. Click Create PDF and download.

Works on all devices

  • Windows (Chrome / Edge)
  • Mac (Safari / Chrome)
  • Android phones (Chrome)
  • iPhone (Safari)

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device.
