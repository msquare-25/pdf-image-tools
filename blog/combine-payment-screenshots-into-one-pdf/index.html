How to Combine Payment Screenshots into One PDF for Reimbursement or Proof

Offices, landlords, accountants, and legal teams usually ask for one single PDF — not 5–10 separate screenshots. This guide shows how to combine payment screenshots from GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, UPI, or bank apps into one clean PDF.

When you need a single payment-proof PDF

  • Office reimbursement claims
  • Rent payment proof for landlord
  • Freelancer invoices & client records
  • Legal or tax documentation
  • Expense tracking and audits

Step-by-step: Combine payment screenshots into one PDF

  1. Open Image → PDF.
  2. Select all payment screenshots (JPG/PNG).
  3. Arrange images in correct date or transaction order.
  4. Generate and download the single PDF file.

All processing happens in your browser — screenshots are not uploaded.

Make your payment PDF look professional

  • Crop extra status bars or blank space using Crop PDF.
  • Reorder pages if transactions are mixed using Reorder PDF.
  • Compress the final file if size is large using Compress PDF.
  • Use a clear filename: April_2025_UPI_Payments.pdf

Why PDF is better than sending screenshots

  • One attachment instead of many
  • Easy to store and forward
  • Looks professional and organized
  • Accepted by HR, CA, and legal teams
