How to Convert Multiple Images to One PDF on Windows & Mac

Combine several JPG, PNG or HEIC images into a single PDF using a fast, free online tool.

Why convert images to a PDF?

A single PDF is easier to share, upload and store than multiple images. Many job portals, government sites, and applications require documents in PDF format.

Steps (Windows / Mac)

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool in your browser.
  2. Click “Upload Images” and select multiple JPG/PNG files.
  3. Reorder the images if needed.
  4. Choose page size (A4 recommended).
  5. Click “Create PDF”.
  6. Download your combined single PDF.

Tips for best output

  • Use high-resolution images for cleaner PDF quality.
  • Use Crop PDF (after merging) to tighten margins.
  • If the final PDF is too large, use Compress PDF.
  • HEIC images from iPhone are supported — they convert smoothly.

Everything runs inside your browser — no installation required.
