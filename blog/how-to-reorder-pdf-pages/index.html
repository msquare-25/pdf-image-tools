How to Reorder PDF Pages (Desktop & Mobile)

Easily rearrange, shuffle, or fix page order in your PDF using a simple drag-and-drop tool — works on all devices.

Why reorder PDF pages?

Sometimes PDF pages get scanned in the wrong order, or you want to change the flow of your document. Instead of re-scanning or using heavy apps, you can fix the page order directly in your browser.

Step-by-step: Reorder PDF pages

  1. Open the Reorder PDF tool.
  2. Upload your PDF file.
  3. Drag and drop page thumbnails into the order you want.
  4. Remove any unwanted pages using the trash icon.
  5. Click “Save & Download” to export your newly ordered PDF.

Works on all devices

  • Windows & Mac — Open in Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox
  • Android — Works in Chrome
  • iPhone — Works in Safari

Everything runs client-side, meaning your file never leaves your device for processing.

Tip: Clean up your PDF before saving

Combine your reorder task with these helpful tools:

  • Delete pages to remove unwanted pages
  • Crop PDF to fix margins
  • Compress PDF to reduce final file size

Once done, your PDF will be neat, organized, and ready for sharing or printing.
