How to Convert Images (JPG/PNG/HEIC) to One PDF on Mobile

Create a single, clean PDF from multiple photos using only your phone browser — no app install required.

Why Combine Photos into a PDF?

Colleges, offices, and portals often prefer a single PDF instead of 5–10 separate images. But your phone camera saves files as JPG, PNG, or HEIC. Converting them into one PDF keeps everything in order and makes uploads much easier.

With the Image → PDF tool, you can do this directly on Android or iPhone.

Steps on Android & iPhone

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool in Chrome, Safari, or any browser.
  2. Tap Upload and select the photos you want (JPG, PNG, HEIC).
  3. Wait for them to appear in the preview list.
  4. Drag to reorder pages so they appear in the right sequence.
  5. Confirm options like page size or orientation if available.
  6. Tap convert/download to get your single PDF file.

Practical Uses

  • Submitting handwritten assignments or notes
  • Sending ID proof, address proof, and photo together
  • Combining bills, receipts, and warranties
  • Sharing pages from a notebook or book scan

After creating the PDF, you can also compress it if a portal has a strict file size limit.
