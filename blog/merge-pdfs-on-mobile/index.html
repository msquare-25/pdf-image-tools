How to Merge PDFs on Mobile (Android & iPhone)

Combine multiple PDFs into one file directly from your phone browser — no app install, no signup.

Can you really merge PDFs on a phone?

Yes. You don’t need a heavy app or laptop. You can upload PDFs from your phone storage, change their order, and download one combined PDF using the Merge PDF tool in your browser.

Steps on Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome on your phone.
  2. Go to the Merge PDF tool.
  3. Tap the upload area and choose PDFs from your device or cloud storage.
  4. Drag to reorder the files if needed.
  5. Tap “Merge & Download” to get a single combined PDF.

Steps on iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Visit the Merge PDF tool.
  3. Tap to upload PDFs from Files or other storage apps.
  4. Adjust the order of documents.
  5. Tap “Merge & Download” and save the new PDF back to Files.

Tips for cleaner merged PDFs

  • Use Delete pages to remove unwanted pages before merging.
  • Use Reorder PDF if you need to adjust page order after merging.
  • Use Compress PDF if the merged file becomes too large to share.

Everything runs in your browser. Your PDFs are processed on your device — no file is stored on our servers.
