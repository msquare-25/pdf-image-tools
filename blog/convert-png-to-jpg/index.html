How to Convert PNG to JPG (Online)

Shrink image size and make uploads easier by turning PNGs into JPGs in your browser.

Why convert PNG to JPG?

PNGs are great for quality and transparency, but they can be large. JPG usually gives a much smaller file size, which is better for uploads, email and websites.

Step-by-step: Convert PNG to JPG

  1. Open the PNG → JPG tool.
  2. Upload one or more PNG images.
  3. Wait for the tool to convert them into JPG format.
  4. Download the JPG files (or a ZIP if there are many).

Tips for smaller but usable images

  • JPG is lossy, so avoid multiple re-saves if you need quality.
  • Keep an original PNG backup if the image is important.
  • If image files are still too big to send, use Compress PDF after turning images into a PDF.

Everything runs in your browser — your images are never stored on our servers.
