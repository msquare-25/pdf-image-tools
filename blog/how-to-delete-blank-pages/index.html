How to Delete Blank Pages from a PDF

Remove empty, blank or unwanted PDF pages quickly using a free browser tool — works on all devices.

Why do blank pages appear?

Blank pages often appear due to scanning errors, merged documents with extra empty pages, or exported PDFs that insert filler pages. You can remove them instantly without re-editing the file.

Step-by-step: Delete blank PDF pages

  1. Open the Delete pages tool.
  2. Upload your PDF file.
  3. Select the blank or unwanted pages.
  4. Click “Delete & Download”.

Tips before deleting

  • Zoom into thumbnails to double-check the page content.
  • Use Rotate PDF if a page looks blank but is rotated sideways.
  • Use Crop PDF to clean scanned edges.

Everything runs inside your browser — your file never uploads to our server.
